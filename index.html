About

I am Associate Professor of Linguistics at the University of Kentucky, specializing in Indo-European historical linguistics and computational methods. My primary research focus is the creation and application of innovative approaches to solve difficult problems in historical linguistics. I also work to educate the public about Proto-Indo-European (PIE) and historical linguistics through constructed languages and other forms of outreach.

This site provides quick access to my CV, research, public engagement, and teaching materials.
