Abridged CV

Andrew Byrd — Historical Linguistics · Conlangs · Computation

Profile

My primary research focus is the creation and application of innovative approaches to solve difficult problems in historical linguistics. I also work to educate the public about Proto-Indo-European (PIE) and historical linguistics through constructed languages and other forms of outreach.
